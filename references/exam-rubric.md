# Exam Rubric Reference

Use this before generating Exam 1, Exam 2, or Exam 3.

## Paper Requirements

Each paper must include:

- student-facing paper without answers,
- separate answer key,
- marking rubric,
- source trace for every question,
- knowledge ID coverage table,
- difficulty mix,
- non-repetition check against previous generated papers.

## Exam Progression

- Exam 1: foundation and broad coverage.
- Exam 2: deliberate practice, S1 priorities, weak points, harder variants.
- Exam 3: sprint diagnostic, comprehensive, slightly harder than expected when pressure simulation is selected.

Harder means deeper reasoning, mixed conditions, better distractors, or tighter time. It does not mean out-of-scope.
